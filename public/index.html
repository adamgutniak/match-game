How to Play

  • Player 1 = X · Player 2 = O. Players alternate turns.
  • Each turn, place your symbol in any cell — or on the center button — that doesn't already have it.
  • A cell is complete (yellow) when it has both X and O.
  • Win by completing 4 in a row in any row, column, or diagonal.
  • The player who places the completing piece wins.
  • Center cooldown: after placing on the center, the opponent must wait one turn.
  • Diagonal bonus: when a diagonal has 3 complete cells and the 4th holds only the opponent's symbol, the center button glows — press it to complete the diagonal and win.
